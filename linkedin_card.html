agentsync · context-keeper · cambium

Three MCPs. One loop.
Knowledge that compounds itself.

Every AI knowledge layer I tried failed the same way: it was a side system nobody's agent ever called. So I stopped building a system and built three small MCPs that live where the work already is — git.

present tense
agentsync
Who's touching what right now, so two agents don't collide.
past tense
context-keeper
Why each decision was made, kept per project.
future tense
cambium
What everyone should carry forward — fed by the other two.
